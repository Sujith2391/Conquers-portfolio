About Me

Hello! I'm Sujith, a passionate e.g., UI/UX Designer, Front-End Developer based in Manipal/India. I specialize in creating clean, intuitive, and user-friendly digital experiences.

My journey in design/development began with a fascination for how people interact with technology. I believe in a user-centered approach, focusing on understanding needs and translating them into elegant and functional solutions.

When I'm not crafting pixels or writing code, you can find me exploring new coffee shops, hiking, reading sci-fi,watching anime. I'm always eager to learn new things and collaborate on exciting projects. Let's build something great together!
